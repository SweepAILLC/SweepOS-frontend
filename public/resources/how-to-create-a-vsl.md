# VSL CREATION

There are many reasons and use cases for a video sales letter. Those being:

- educate
- nurture
- convert

However depending on what funnel you run and what your content strategy currently looks like, you’ll need to structure your VSL differently. Below are proven frameworks you can steal when building your VSL.

## Traditional VSL:

Hey! Your probably watching this because [INSERT PAIN POINT] and your trying [INSERT ICP CONDITIONS]. I’m [INSERT BREIF INTRO + AUTHORITY]. 

But its not your fault, you just haven’t learned how to [INSERT DREAM OUTCOME] without [INSERT USP]. 

Now this system is exactly how I helped [INSERT CLIENT] achieve [OUTCOME] even while they [INSERT SALES OBJECTION]. 

Its also how I helped [INSERT CLIENT] achieve [OUTCOME] even while they [INSERT SALES OBJECTION]. 

And even how [INSERT CLIENT] achieved [OUTCOME] even while they [INSERT SALES OBJECTION]. 

Now, how do I actually accomplish this for [INSERT SUCCESS STAT]? They all have X things in common

1. INSERT ICP/DREAM CLIENT TRAIT
2. INSERT ICP/DREAM CLIENT TRAIT
3. INSERT ICP/DREAM CLIENT TRAIT

These lay the groundwork for everything I help build on top of. When clients start with me they get…

INSERT DELIVERABLES (NO PRICE) AND ASSOCIATE WITH COMMON ICP GOALS
ex. bi-weekly 1:1 consultations with me and weekly consultations with my CSM to ensure you feel supported and clear on the next steps to scale your business to $10k/mo

So if that sounds at all interesting and you want to learn more, [INSERT CTA]
