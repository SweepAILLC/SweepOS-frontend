Your content funnel is dependent on you creating content that resonates with your ICP at every point in their buyers journey. Through top of funnel (TOF), middle of funnel (MOF), and bottom of funnel (BOF). 

Lets start by defining each stage:

TOF = the least aware a prospect can be of you, your brand, and your service

- often associated with new followers, views, and “viral optimized” content
- Content ideas: viral trends, popular audios, reels

MOF = prospects that are warming up to you as a person Building Your Brand and what you do

- often associated with existing followers, saves, people looking for value/education, building trust + authority, and those invested in your brad
- Content ideas: educational/value content mixed with your personal story, carousels, step by step videos, breakdowns, green screens, raw explanations

BOF = the most aware a prospect can be before buying your product or service

- often associated with story viewers, those who comment, those who reply, or those who reach out
- Content ideas: proof based videos, client testimonials, client stories, client results, offer breakdown, insights into the use of your product or service

Now that you understand each stage applying each content format is crucial to both Building Your Brand but also generating clients. Image your leading a follower down a path to becoming a customer but you have to move at their pace. But in reality you need to lead a social media audience of often hundreds if not thousands down this same path. It would be impossible to sync all of their buying timelines and behaviors. Thus, you need to constantly create content that touches on every phase of the funnel in order to effectively fill your pipeline with leads on a regular basis.

Use these general guidelines when your first starting off but start getting creative and mixing things up but making sure thingd are intentional and optimized with ICP behavioral influence as the goal (reference Defining Your ICP):

TOF: 

- find competitors in your nice, copy their winning videos, and repurpose for your style and brand
- use templates of viral videos and spin them to your ICP
- use trending audios and b-roll clips for quick watch throughs

**Focus on reels/short form videos

MOF:

- break down your expertise into simple frameworks easily explained using the following formats in conjunction with your personal spin (stories, branding, and style):
    - carousels
    - how to videos
    - green screen
    - whiteboard
    - raw explanation

BOF: 

- Clip up client check in calls
- request testimonial videos from clients
- request client transformation photos or proof of progress
- Tell the story of your best ICP client experiences
