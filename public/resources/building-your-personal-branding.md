# BUILDING YOUR PERSONAL BRAND

## Introduction

Your brand is comprised of 3 components:

1. Your story
2. The problem you solve
3. The person you solve it for

This exercise is designed to help you uncover what makes your brand 1/1 and how to leverage it in building a social image optimized for conversions.
