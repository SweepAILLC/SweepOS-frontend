# WHAT IS MARKET RESEARCH?

**Understand exactly what product your ideal clients are looking for**

- **If you haven’t sold, you NEED to perform this part to perfection**

**And even if you already have a product, you still NEED to do market research to get a full understanding of product market fit**

**What is product market fit?**

**Creating a product that a market segment absolutely wants and desires**

**Step By Step Process**

**1)  Book a free 20 minute call with your followers and offer something free**

**Could be early access, story shoutout, etc**

**Ask them these questions to get a good understanding your demographic**

- **What they struggle with right now and where they want to be**
- **Short story about themselves. What happened in the past that led you to where you are now? What major life events lead to interest in TOPIC?**
- **What’s preventing them from accomplishing their goals on their own?**
- **We’re thinking about creating an exclusive community with people just like you with PAIN POINT to get DESIRED RESULT. What would you like about that community?**
- **What could we add to the community that would make it extremely valuable?**
- **How much would you pay for a program like this?**
- **What would someone pay more for?**
- **What would make you want to pay more?**

**Fill these questions out during or after the call.**
