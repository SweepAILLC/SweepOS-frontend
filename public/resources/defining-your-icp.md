# DEFINING YOUR ICP (IDEAL CLIENT PROFILE)

A practical exercise to identify exactly who you serve and how to reach them

---

## What This Is

**What you'll have after:** A crystal-clear profile of your ideal client that informs your content, messaging, offers, and sales process.

---

## Why This Matters

**Vague ICP = Vague messaging = Low conversions**

If you're speaking to "anyone who wants to get in shape," you're speaking to no one. Specificity creates resonance. Resonance creates sales.

**Your goal:** Narrow your focus so tight that your ideal client reads your content and thinks, "This person is talking directly to me."

## Section 8: Validation Check

Answer these honestly. If you answer "no" to any, your ICP isn't tight enough yet.

☐ **Can I visualize this person in my head?** (Name, face, daily routine)

☐ **Do I know where they spend time online?** (Specific platforms, influencers, communities)

☐ **Can I write content that makes them say "This is me"?**

☐ **Do I know their exact words when describing their problem?**

☐ **Can I predict their objections before they say them?**

If you checked all 5 → **You're ready to build content, offers, and funnels that convert.**

If you missed any → **Go back and get more specific.** Talk to 5-10 current or past clients. Ask them these questions directly.

---

## What to Do With This

**Immediate actions:**

1. **Update your Instagram bio** to speak directly to this person
2. **Audit your last 10 posts** – do they resonate with this ICP?
3. **Rewrite your lead magnet pitch** using their exact pain points and language
4. **Create 3 pieces of content** that address their latent conditions or core pains
5. **Adjust your sales call script** to mirror their buying behavior

**Your ICP is not static.** Revisit this every 6 months or whenever you notice your clients shifting.
